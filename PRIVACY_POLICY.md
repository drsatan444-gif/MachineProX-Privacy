# Privacy Policy for MachineProX Calculator

**Last Updated:** November 8, 2025
**Effective Date:** November 8, 2025

## Overview

MachineProX Calculator ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our approach to privacy and data handling for our CNC machining calculator application.

**Key Points:**
- ✅ **No data collection** - We don't collect any personal information
- ✅ **No third-party sharing** - We don't share data because we don't collect it
- ✅ **No analytics or tracking** - No usage analytics, crash reporting, or user tracking
- ✅ **No ads** - The app is ad-free
- ✅ **Offline-first** - All calculations and data stay on your device

## Information We Don't Collect

MachineProX Calculator is designed with privacy in mind. We DO NOT collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Cookies or tracking technologies
- Any form of user-generated content uploaded to servers

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

All data created by the App is stored locally on your device:

- **Calculation History**: Your calculation history is saved in a local database on your device
- **Favorites**: Saved calculations are stored locally
- **Settings**: App preferences are stored in your device's local storage
- **Material & Tool Data**: Reference databases are bundled with the app

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

### Data Deletion

To delete all app data:
1. Uninstall the app from your device, OR
2. Go to your device Settings → Apps → MachineProX Calculator → Storage → Clear Data

This will permanently remove all locally stored calculations, favorites, and settings.

## Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

### Storage Access (Optional)
- **Purpose**: To export calculation results as CSV or PDF files
- **Usage**: Only when you explicitly choose to export/share results
- **Data Handling**: Files are saved to your device's storage; we cannot access them

The App functions fully without any permissions. Permissions are only requested when you use specific features that require them.

## Third-Party Services

MachineProX Calculator does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations
- No cloud storage services
- No crash reporting tools

## Network Communication

The App does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers. All functionality is available offline.

## Children's Privacy

MachineProX Calculator does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any users.

## Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our services. All your calculation data remains on your device, protected by your device's security measures.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Since the App doesn't collect data or connect to the internet, it cannot notify you of policy changes. Please check this policy periodically if you have concerns.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data:
- There is no data to access, correct, or delete from our servers
- There is no data to port to other services
- There are no marketing communications to opt out of

You have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device.
